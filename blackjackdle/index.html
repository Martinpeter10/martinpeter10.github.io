How to Play

Beat the dealer in 3 daily hands of blackjack.

For Entertainment Only

This is a free daily game. No real money is wagered, won, or lost. Must be 18+ to play.

Rules

  • You get 3 hands per day with a standard 52-card deck (reshuffled each hand).
  • Start with 1,000 chips (or your cumulative total). Place a bet before each hand.
  • Hit to draw a card, Stay to hold.
  • Double Down doubles your bet and draws exactly one more card.
  • Split when dealt a pair - play two separate hands (bet is matched on the second).
  • Dealer hits on 16 and below, stays on 17 and above (hits soft 17).
  • Blackjack (21 on first two cards) pays 3:2.

Scoring

Your chip total carries over between days. You receive bonus chips each day to keep playing. Try to build the biggest stack!
