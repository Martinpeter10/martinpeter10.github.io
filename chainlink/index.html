How to Play

Complete the chain by finding each missing word.

Each answer forms a compound word or phrase with the word above it - then becomes the start of the next link. A clue is available if you need it.

Example

Scoring

Each word is worth up to 3 pts. Guess correctly with no hints for 3 pts. Press Hint (or guess wrong) to reveal the first letter - guess right for 2 pts. Press Show Clue (or guess wrong again) to reveal the phrase clue - guess right for 1 pt. Guess wrong with the clue showing or skip at any time to score 0 pts. Solve all 5 without any hints or wrong guesses for a perfect 15 + 5 bonus = 20! 🌟
