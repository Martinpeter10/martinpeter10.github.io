How to Play

Complete the chain by finding each missing word.

Scoring

Each word starts worth 3 pts. Click Clue before guessing and your max drops to 2 pts. Guess wrong and the clue is revealed automatically — get it right next for 1 pt. Guess wrong again with the clue showing and the answer is filled in for 0 pts. Skip any time to score 0. Solve all 5 without hints for a perfect 15!
