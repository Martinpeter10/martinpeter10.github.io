How to Play

Place your bets and spin the wheel - 3 times per day!

For Entertainment Only

This is a free daily game. No real money is wagered, won, or lost. Must be 18+ to play.

How to Bet

  • Select a chip denomination (10, 25, 50, 100, or ALL IN), then tap any bet on the board.
  • You can place multiple bets at once across different areas - they all ride the same spin.
  • Tap a bet area again to add more chips to it. Tap Clear to remove all bets.
  • Hit Spin when you're ready!
  • Tap the bar chart button (next to ?) at any time to view your spin history, red/black & odd/even breakdowns, and how long it's been since a green.
