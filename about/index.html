About DailyJamm

DailyJamm is a daily games hub built for people who love quick, fun daily challenges. We build our own original games and curate a collection of the best daily games on the web - all in one place.

Our Games

Themedle - A daily TV show theme song guessing game. Listen to a short clip and guess the show in up to 6 attempts. Each wrong guess reveals a longer clip.

Chain Link - A daily compound word chain puzzle. Find the 5 missing words that link the chain. Use up to two hints per word (first letter, then a phrase clue) - or go hint-free for a perfect 20!

BlackJackdle - Daily blackjack with 3 hands per day. Start with 1,000 chips and a daily bonus, then beat the dealer to build your stack.

Spelldle - A daily D&D 5E spell guessing game. Identify the spell in up to 8 tries using hints on level, school, range, components, class, and more.

Roulettedle - Daily American roulette with 3 spins per day. Place bets on numbers, colors, odd/even, dozens, columns, and more - then spin the wheel and see where the ball lands.

Holdle - Daily Texas Hold'em poker across 3 hands. Face off against 3 randomly selected AI opponents - each with a distinct personality - and see how many chips you can win.

Our Mission

We believe the best daily games are simple, satisfying, and over in a few minutes. DailyJamm exists to make finding and playing them effortless - no accounts, no downloads, no friction.
