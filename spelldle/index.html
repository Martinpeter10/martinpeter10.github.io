    How to Play

    Guess the daily D&D 5E spell in up to 8 tries.

    Type a spell name and press Guess. After each guess, each attribute tile will reveal how close you are:

    3rd Green — exact match
    2nd ↑ Yellow — close (level within ±2, range/duration adjacent tier). Arrow shows which direction the answer is.
    Illusion Gray — not a match

    The 9 attributes:

    • Level — Cantrip through 9th
    • School — e.g. Evocation, Illusion
    • Casting Time — Action, Bonus, Reaction
    • Range — Self / Touch / 30 ft / 60–120 ft / 150+ ft / Special
    • Components — V, S, M or combinations
    • Concentration — Yes or No
    • Ritual — Yes or No
    • Duration — from Instant to Permanent
    • Class — which D&D classes can cast it
