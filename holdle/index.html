How to Play

Beat 3 AI opponents in 3 daily hands of Texas Hold'em.

For Entertainment Only

This is a free daily game. No real money is wagered, won, or lost. Must be 18+ to play.

Rules

  • You get 3 hands per day against 3 AI opponents.
  • Start with 1,000 chips (or your cumulative total). Place a bet before each hand.
  • Each hand plays out with a flop (3 cards), turn (1 card), and river (1 card).
  • You can Fold, Call, Check, or Raise at each betting round.
  • Best 5-card hand wins the pot. At showdown, all AI cards are revealed.
  • You are always the dealer/button - you act last pre-flop, first post-flop.

Today's Opponents

The Opponents

Each AI opponent has a unique play style - pay attention and figure out their tendencies as you play. Part of the challenge is reading the table!

Scoring

Your chip total carries over between days. You receive bonus chips each day to keep playing. Try to build the biggest stack!

Hand Rankings

Best to worst. In a tie, the higher ranked hand wins.

1
Royal Flush
A♥K♥Q♥J♥10♥

Ace, King, Queen, Jack, 10 - all the same suit. The best possible hand.

2
Straight Flush
9♠8♠7♠6♠5♠

Five consecutive cards all of the same suit.

3
Four of a Kind
K♠K♥K♦K♣A♥

All four cards of the same rank. The highest rank wins ties.

4
Full House
Q♠Q♥Q♦8♣8♦

Three of a kind combined with a pair. The trips rank decides ties.

5
Flush
A♦J♦8♦5♦2♦

Any five cards of the same suit. Highest card decides ties.

6
Straight
8♠7♥6♦5♣4♠

Five cards in consecutive order, any suits. Ace can be high or low.

7
Three of a Kind
J♠J♥J♦A♥7♠

Three cards of the same rank. Higher rank beats lower rank.

8
Two Pair
A♥A♠9♣9♦K♠

Two different pairs. The higher pair decides ties.

9
One Pair
10♠10♦A♥K♣7♠

Two cards of the same rank. Higher pair wins; kicker cards break ties.

10
High Card
A♦Q♠9♥6♣3♦

No combination. The highest card in your hand plays.
